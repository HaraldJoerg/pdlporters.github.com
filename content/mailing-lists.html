PDL Mailing Lists

If you are interested in contacting other people who are using PDL, if you need help for a special problem, or if you are a developer and want to help us improving PDL, you should subscribe to one of our mailing lists. There are two lists (thanks to Frossie), one to talk about PDL and one for developers to coordinate their work.

  1. The generalmailing list is perldl@jach.hawaii.edu. This list is intended for general user questions, non-technical discussions about PDL and announcements. To subscribe, fill out this form.
  2. The second list is the developer's mailing list. This can get very technical. The list address is pdl-porters@jach.hawaii.edu . Fill out this form to subscribe.

Archives

Searchable archives of both the perldl and pdl-porters mailing lists are available for the period 1996-2004 thanks to Achim Bohnet. More recent messages (since June 2005) for the perldl and pdl-porters lists are separately searchable. For convenience, you can search the more recent messages using the following form.


perldl pdl-porters
