PDL Mailing Lists

If you are interested in contacting other people who are using PDL, if you need help for a special problem, or if you are a developer and want to help us improving PDL, you should subscribe to one of our mailing lists. There are two lists (thanks to Frossie), one to talk about PDL and one for developers to coordinate their work.

1) The general mailing list is perldl@jach.hawaii.edu. This list is intended for general user questions, non-technical discussions about PDL and announcements. To subscribe, fill out this form.

2) The second list is the developer's mailing list. This can get very technical. The list address is pdl-porters@jach.hawaii.edu. Fill out this form to subscribe.
